# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"Space Repair Assist: Revolutionizing remote server repair in space with cutting-edge AR/VR guidance and on-demand 3D printing capabilities."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. "Empowering space engineers with immersive repair simulations and instant fabrication tools for seamless mission success."
2. "Transforming space maintenance with innovative virtual guidance and rapid prototyping solutions for critical repairs."
3. "Your ultimate partner in space mission reliability, providing real-time support and custom tool creation at your fingertips."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Dr. Emily Carter**  
   - **Daily Job:** Lead Systems Engineer at a space agency  
   - **Budget:** $250,000 annually for tools and software  
   
2. **Persona 2: Captain Mark Thompson**  
   - **Daily Job:** Mission Commander for manned space missions  
   - **Budget:** $150,000 annually for mission support technologies  

3. **Persona 3: Sarah Lee**  
   - **Daily Job:** Operations Manager at a satellite repair company  
   - **Budget:** $100,000 annually for operational efficiency tools  

## Channels
1. **Direct Sales:**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $15,000 per customer  
   - Focus on building relationships with space agencies and private space companies.

2. **Industry Conferences and Trade Shows:**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $10,000 per customer  
   - Attend and present at key aerospace and technology events to showcase the product.

3. **Content Marketing and SEO:**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $5,000 per customer  
   - Develop targeted content to attract organic traffic from relevant searches related to space repair solutions.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog post introducing Space Repair Assist; share on social media.
- **Week 2:** Publish a case study on the importance of remote repair in space missions.
- **Week 3:** Host a webinar featuring industry experts discussing challenges in space maintenance.
- **Week 4:** Release an infographic on the benefits of AR/VR in technical training.
- **Week 5:** Share testimonials from beta users and early adopters.
- **Week 6:** Create a video demo of Space Repair Assist in action.
- **Week 7:** Publish an article on future trends in space technology and maintenance.
- **Week 8:** Launch a targeted email campaign to ICP personas.
- **Week 9:** Engage with industry influencers for guest blog posts and social media mentions.
- **Week 10:** Release a whitepaper on cost savings through effective space repair solutions.
- **Week 11:** Host a Q&A session on social media to address user queries.
- **Week 12:** Compile and share a summary of the first quarter's achievements and insights.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and complete beta testing; prepare marketing materials.
- **D-0:** Official product launch; execute launch event and press release.
- **D+30:** Gather user feedback and iterate on product based on initial usage data.
- **D+90:** Evaluate market penetration and adjust marketing strategies based on performance metrics.

## Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target 1,000 DAUs by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue ($MRR):** Target $100,000 MRR by D+90.
