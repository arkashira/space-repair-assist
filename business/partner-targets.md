## Partner Integration Roadmap for Space Repair Assist

### Rationale for Selection

1. **Slack**: Essential for fostering communication among teams, especially when remote guidance is required during repairs. The integration allows for seamless sharing of updates and instructions.

2. **Zoom**: Critical for providing live support and guidance through video calls, enabling engineers to receive real-time assistance as they perform repairs.

3. **Trello**: Helps in organizing and managing repair tasks, ensuring that all team members are aligned on priorities and deadlines.

4. **GitHub**: Vital for managing code related to repair simulations and documentation, facilitating collaboration among developers and engineers.

5. **AWS IoT**: While it has a higher integration effort, it provides significant value by enabling real-time data collection and monitoring of equipment in space, which is crucial for effective repairs.

6. **Twilio**: Enhances communication capabilities by allowing the sending of alerts and updates, ensuring engineers are informed of any changes or critical information during repairs.

7. **Unity**: Provides a platform for developing AR/VR applications that can simulate repair scenarios, making training more effective and engaging.

8. **Zapier**: Automates repetitive tasks and connects various tools, streamlining workflows and improving efficiency in the repair process.

### Conclusion

The selected partners and integrations are aimed at enhancing the functionality of the Space Repair Assist tool, addressing the specific needs of space mission operators and engineers. Prioritizing those with affiliate or revenue-share opportunities will also help in generating additional revenue streams.
