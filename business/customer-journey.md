# Customer Journey Map for Space Repair Assist

## Phase 1: Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Announcement of a new space mission requiring advanced repair capabilities.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about available tools; existing solutions are not tailored for space environments.
- **User Emotions**: Curiosity mixed with frustration; need for innovative solutions.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content showcasing the benefits of VR/AR in space repair; webinars featuring experts in space engineering.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement with educational content.

## Phase 2: Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Researching tools for remote guidance and simulation for space repairs.
- **Friction Points**: Difficulty in comparing existing solutions; uncertainty about the effectiveness of new technology in a space context.
- **User Emotions**: Skepticism and cautious optimism; desire for reassurance and validation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Case studies demonstrating successful applications; testimonials from industry experts; interactive demos of the product.
- **Metric**: Number of inquiries and demo requests.

## Phase 3: Try
- **Trigger Event**: Accessing a trial version of the Space Repair Assist tool.
- **Friction Points**: Technical issues during setup; steep learning curve for new technology.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with frustration; eagerness to see results but hindered by usability challenges.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized onboarding sessions; responsive customer support to assist during trial.
- **Metric**: Trial activation rate and user engagement metrics during the trial period.

## Phase 4: Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Successful completion of the trial period with positive outcomes.
- **Friction Points**: Budget constraints; internal approval processes for new tool adoption.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction with the product; anxiety about securing budget and approvals.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Flexible pricing models; assistance with internal presentations to stakeholders; highlighting ROI from trial usage.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription.

## Phase 5: Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Positive feedback from initial use leads to interest in additional features or services.
- **Friction Points**: Potential resistance to change from team members; integration with existing systems.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence in the product; enthusiasm for expanding capabilities but cautious about change management.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offering tailored solutions based on user feedback; providing training sessions for expanded features; showcasing success stories from other teams.
- **Metric**: Upsell rate and customer satisfaction scores post-expansion.
